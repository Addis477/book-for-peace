The Beginning

It started with a simple observation: everyone looks at the same sky. Whether in Addis Ababa or Tokyo, Nairobi or London, the sky connects us all.

In 2026, Peace for You Network was born from this truth. Not as another social platform, but as a living space where civil voices from every country could share one word: their peace.

"One civil voice from every country"

195 countries. 8 billion people. One network of peace.

The Sky Philosophy

The sky asks nothing of us. It simply exists — beautiful, changing, eternal. Peace for You Network is built on this same principle.
